Google Meet is a video conferencing platform developed by Google, designed for virtual meetings, online collaboration, and remote communication. It allows users to host or join meetings with features like screen sharing, real-time captions, breakout rooms, and background noise reduction. Integrated seamlessly with Google Workspace, Google Meet enables businesses, educators, and individuals to conduct secure and high-quality video calls across devices. It supports large meetings, offering functionalities like recording, live streaming, and multi-device access, making it a reliable choice for professional and personal communication.
